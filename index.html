Welcome!

Hi there! We are Mukilteo Robotics from Mukilteo, Washington. We are a student-run organization that currently participates in VRC of the VEX Robotics program. Throughout our 2 years of operation, we have experienced many successes that have made us one of the most prestigious robotics clubs in Washington State.

Coding

Programming is a core piece for our Robotics Teams. Programmers use software tools to produce code that is used in both driver control and autonomous operation. Our programmers currently work (and will most likely forever be working) on odometry, computer vision, and basic turning.

Building

Building and designing the robots that compete in the games is an integral piece of our robotics program. Student engineers design, document, and construct robots and components from a variety of tools and electronics. Though lacking in efficiency and available parts, our builders boast excellence in ambition, motivation, and yoinking other team's designs.
